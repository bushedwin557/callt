Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our catering services

We recommend booking our services at least 2-4 weeks in advance for small events and 2-3 months for larger events like weddings. However, we understand that last-minute needs can arise, so please contact us even for short-notice requests, and we'll do our best to accommodate you.

Yes, we can accommodate various dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and allergy-specific requirements. Please inform us of any dietary restrictions when planning your event, and we'll work with you to create appropriate menu options.

Our standard catering package includes food preparation, delivery, setup, and cleanup. Depending on your needs, we can also provide servers, bartenders, tableware, linens, and other event essentials. We'll provide a detailed quote based on your specific requirements.

Yes, we offer menu tastings for clients planning larger events like weddings or corporate functions. Tastings are typically scheduled after an initial consultation and are subject to availability. Please contact us for more information about scheduling a tasting.

Our cancellation policy varies depending on the size of the event and how far in advance the cancellation is made. Generally, cancellations made at least 14 days before the event date will receive a full refund of the deposit, minus any non-refundable fees. Please refer to your contract for specific terms.
