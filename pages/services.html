Our Catering Process

We make planning your event simple and stress-free

01

Initial Consultation

We start by talking to you at length about your event, likes, dislikes, budget, vision, etc. This can be done on the phone, by video call, or in-person at our office.

02

Menu Planning

Based on your taste, dietary needs, and event theme, our chefs prepare a bespoke menu for you. We do menu tastings so that everything is just the way you like.

03

Event Coordination

From staffing and equipment rentals to planning out timelines and relying on other vendors, we take care of everything so no I goes undotted and T un-crossed.

04

Preparation

Using only the freshest and finest of ingredients, our culinary staff serves the same great food they have always made during your event.

05

Day-of Execution

Our professional staff arrives at your venue plenty of time to set up. This comes with a full-service food based service to ensure everything runs on time.

06

Clean-up & Follow-up

Clear it away and follow up after to make sure you were completely satisfied.
